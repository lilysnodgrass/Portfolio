About Lily

Born and raised Austinite who rides a 2016 Moto Guzzi v7ii Stone, Lily received her BFA in Studio Art from Texas State Univeristy in 2017 with Magna Cum Laude honors. Majoring in drawing, Lily's thesis focused on female motorcyclists and the rampant sexism within the culture around motorcycles. She is a driven and passionate creative, with a diverse work experience.
